CHSS Supplementary Items

Supplementary Medical Items under CHSS : The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) supports its employeesâ€™ health and well-being by providing supplementary medical items under its comprehensive healthcare policy. These provisions aim to ensure employees have access to necessary medical aids beyond routine care.

* Key Benefits :

  • Reimbursement: Costs for specific supplementary medical items prescribed by authorized medical officers are reimbursed
  • Coverage: Includes items such as Nebulizer devices, CPAP machine, Hearing aids and other prescribed medical accessories
  • Eligibility: Applicable to SAC employees and their dependent family members as per ISRO medical policy
  • Ease of Access: Claims are processed through a structured procedure with the required prescriptions and receipts
Glucometer :
  • One Per Family, Life Time.
  • Recommendation is required by Panel Specialist.
  • The item should be purchased from licensed shop (Shop Tin No. required).
  • Claim in Form No. 7, countersigned by AMO with prescription and bill in original.
  • Amount Limit : The Maximum Reimbursement is Rs.1,200/-
Glucometer Strips :
  • 100 Per Beneficiary / 150 per Juvenile in a year.
  • Recommendation is required by Panel Specialist.
  • The item should be purchased from licensed shop (Shop Tin No. required).
  • Claim in Form No. 7, countersigned by AMO with prescription and bill in original.
  • 10 needles per month and 20 BD syringes per month per patient is allowed.
Insulin Pen :
  • One Per Beneficiary, Life Time or when replacement required.
  • Recommendation is required by Panel Endocrinologist.
  • The item should be purchased from licensed shop (Shop Tin No. required).
  • Claim in Form No. 7, countersigned by AMO with prescription and bill in original.
CPAP Machine :
  • One Per Beneficiary, Life Time. (Need Prior Approval)
  • Recommendation is required by Panel Pulmonologist.
  • The item should be purchased from licensed shop (Shop Tin No. required).
  • Claim in Form No. 7, countersigned by AMO with prescription and bill in original.
  • Amount Limit : The Maximum Reimbursement is Rs.45,000/-
Nebulizer Machine :
  • One Per Family, Life Time.
  • Recommendation is required by Panel Specialist.
  • The item should be purchased from licensed shop (Shop Tin No. required).
  • Claim in Form No. 7, countersigned by AMO with prescription and bill in original.
  • Amount Limit : The Maximum Reimbursement is Rs.1,500/-
Analog / Digital Hearing AID - Unilateral or Bilateral :
  • One Pair Per Beneficiary, Life Time.
  • Three Audiogram reports from 3 different Panel ENT Specialist and then prescription from AMO is required.
  • The item may be bought from CHSS empanelled Seller : Dr. B Mallikajun, PHC Hearing Aids Care, 25 Palm Spring, 2nd Floor, Ellisbridge, Near Parimal Garden, Ahmedabad, Phone - 26461963
  • Claim in Form No. 7, countersigned by AMO with prescription and bill in original.
  • Amount Limit : One side hearing aid (body worn/pocket/BTE or in the canal type)(conventional) - Rs.10000/-
  • Amount Limit : Bilateral hearing aid - Rs.20000/-
  • Amount Limit : Digital hearing aid (each ear,once in a lifetime) - Rs.30000/-
  • Amount Limit : Ear mould for each ear - Rs.100/-
Drug Eluding Stents :
  • Three Per Beneficiary, Life Time (Additional with approval of office).
  • Recommendation is required by Panel Cardiologist.
  • Claim directly by hospital.
  • Amount Limit : The Maximum Reimbursement is Rs.95,000/- per Stent.
In Vitro Fertilization :
  • Recommendation is required by Panel Gynecologist.
  • Claim directly by Hospital/Specialist.
  • Amount Limit : The Maximum Reimbursement is Rs.65,000/- per cycle.
