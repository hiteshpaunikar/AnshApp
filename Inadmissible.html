Inadmissible Medicines/Items List under CHSS:
  • Patient comfort kit, Bath wet wipes, Bath towels, Tissue papers, ID Band and Footwear.
  • Pad, Underpad, Sanitary pad, Diaper (admissible during hospitalization only)
  • Hand Sanitizer/Medicated soaps
  • Spittoon/ Urinal Can/ Bed pan/ Clinical Thermometers/ Urometer
  • All topical preparations for Hair growth (eg. Minoxidil etc.)
  • All Sunscreen, Moisturizer preparations (even if prescribed by Dermatologist)
  • All cosmetic skin care products
  • Anti-obesity drugs
  • Disposable Insulin Pens (except if not available in any other form)
  • Nutritional and food Supplements
  • All herbal preparations in oral or topical form S Adenosyl Methionine (eg. Heptral), Alpha Keto-Analogue, Methionine, Coenzyme Q10, Collagen peptide, L-Carnitine, Calcitonin Nasal Spray, Evening
  • Primrose Oil (eg. Primosa, GLA 120, etc.), Costly Probiotic like VSL3.
  • All multi-vitamins, mineral and anti-oxidant preparations except, iron, calcium, vitamin A, plain B complex, Folic acid, B12, C and D.
  • Mixed preparations containing ayurvedic, herbal and allopathic drugs
  • Ayurvedic/Homeopathic preparations prescribed by allopathic doctors
  • Allopathic preparations prescribed by doctors practicing any other systems of medicine
  • Sublingual oral vaccine for allergy
  • Dental Implants
  • Soaps, Face washes, Shampoos, Cleansing agents, whitening creams, Mouth washes, Toothpastes (Medicated/Non-medicated, even if, prescribed by Dentist) etc.,
  • Any other items as specified by DOS from time to time
*Please contact to CHSS Section for further information.
