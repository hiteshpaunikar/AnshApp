ISRO Guest House List

ISRO Guest House Facility : The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) provides guest house accommodations to its employees and retired employees across various cities in India. These facilities are designed to offer a comfortable and convenient stay, primarily for employees on official duty, training, or transit.

* Key Features :

  • Well-furnished rooms with essential amenities.
  • Dining facilities with homely meals
  • Affordable charges with priority given to official bookings.

* Eligibility :

  • ISRO employees (current and retired).
  • Accommodation is subject to availability and must be booked in advance through the concerned ISRO center.
